Your AI email assistant
Profitable SaaS with $36,000 in TTM revenue and $34,000 in TTM profit that learns from your writing style and crafts email replies as if they were written by you. It is available as a Chrome or Firefox extension, and works natively in Gmail, Fastmail, and by a popup in any other browser tab. It works by injecting buttons into the Gmail and Fastmail reply box. Using these buttons the user can generate replies to the current email in various different tones and expressions. ✅ $36,000 in TTM revenue ✅ $34,000 in TTM profit ✅ $3,000 in revenue last month ✅ $2,900 in profit last month ✅ Competitors: Copy, Compose The user can provide training data so that it knows how to format and phrase the emails.
